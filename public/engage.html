Sponsor a Thanksgiving meal for a needy family.

Will you provide a Thanksgiving meal for a needy family in the community?

  • Fill a basket with all the trimmings for a traditional Thanksgiving meal (minus perishables)
  • Add a grocery store gift card (Giant or Food Lion) so they can purchase the turkey and other perishable items for the meal. ($25 - $30)
  • ALTERNATIVE -- Purchase a grocery store gift card from Giant or Food Lion ($75 - $100)
  • Include a note card with a prayer or words of encouragement.
  • Include your favorite recipe (OPTIONAL) -- be sure to include the non-perishable ingredients in the basket.
Drop the basket off at the church before November 22. (Call Richard Brash 410-292-4352 to make arrangements)
