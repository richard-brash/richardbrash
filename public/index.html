Where is Curacao?

Curacao is a small island in the Caribbean, just off the coast of Venezuela. It is part of the ABC islands (Aruba, Curacao, Bonaire)

Stay informed »

What kind of place is Curacao?.

Curacao is a constituent country of the Kingdom of the Netherlands. The relationship between Curacao and the Kingdom of the Netherlands is similar to the relationship between the US and Puerto Rico.

Curacao is a polyglot society (multiple languages). The official language is Dutch but the common language is Papiamentu. Most islanders speak more than 1 language and English very popular.

Stay informed »

Tell me more.

The Island is 5 miles wide, 23 miles long and has a tropical savannah climate. The temperatures are relatively constant with little variations through the year with an average range of 80 in “winter” and 88 in “summer”.

Stay informed »

Tell me even more.

It is a major cruise destination with ships arriving and departing every day. There is a vibrant shopping district that serves the tourist industry. The shops are owned by a cross section of the society with the larger percentage belonging to the more affluent Dutch nationals.

  There are many Dutch nationals living on the Island in various communities that are some of the most affluent on the island.
